⚖️ Protect Your Brand Legally

Medium Priority

Ensure your brand name is legally available and protect it from competitors.

1

Search Existing Trademarks

Check if your name is already trademarked by someone else

🇺🇸 USPTO Database 🌍 WIPO Global Database
2

Assess Similar Names

Look for names that sound similar or could cause confusion

⚠️ Even slight variations can lead to legal disputes
3

Consult a Trademark Attorney

Get professional advice before filing your trademark

💼 Recommended $500-$2000
4

File Your Trademark

Register your trademark in your country and internationally if needed

💰 Filing fees: $250-$400 per class (USPTO)
