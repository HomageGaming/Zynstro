Step 1: Create EmailJS Account

  1. Go to EmailJS.com
  2. Click "Sign Up" (FREE - No credit card required)
  3. Verify your email address

Step 2: Add Email Service

  1. In EmailJS dashboard, go to Email Services
  2. Click "Add New Service"
  3. Choose Gmail, Outlook, or any SMTP service
  4. Follow setup wizard and connect your email
  5. Copy the Service ID (e.g., service_abc123)

Step 3: Create Email Template

  1. Go to Email Templates
  2. Click "Create New Template"
  3. Set Subject: Verify Your Zynstro Account
  4. Use these template variables:
Hello {{to_name}}, Your Zynstro verification code is: {{verification_code}} This code expires in 10 minutes. Best regards, The Zynstro Team
  1. Copy the Template ID (e.g., template_xyz789)

Step 4: Get Your Public Key

  1. In EmailJS, go to Account ‚Üí General
  2. Find your Public Key
  3. Copy it (e.g., abcDEF123xyz)

Step 5: Configure Zynstro

Open script.js and update this section:

const EMAILJS_CONFIG = { publicKey: 'YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY_HERE', serviceID: 'YOUR_SERVICE_ID_HERE', templateID: 'YOUR_TEMPLATE_ID_HERE' };

Replace the yellow placeholders with your actual values from EmailJS.

Step 6: Test It!

  1. Save script.js
  2. Refresh index.html in your browser
  3. Click "Sign Up" and use your real email
  4. Check your inbox for the verification code
  5. Enter the code to complete registration
